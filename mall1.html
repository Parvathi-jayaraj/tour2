Shopping Malls

'HiLITE Mall'

HiLITE Mall is one of the largest and most well-known shopping centers in Kozhikode. Located in the northern part of the city, it offers a world-class shopping experience with a blend of luxury brands, popular retail chains, and entertainment options. The mall features a wide range of stores, from fashion and electronics to accessories and home décor. HiLITE Mall also includes a large food court with various dining options, along with a multiplex cinema. The mall’s spacious design and modern facilities make it a popular destination for both locals and tourists. The presence of a large parking area and excellent connectivity add to its appeal.
Opening Hours: Sunday to Saturday:09:30 am - 11:00 pm

'Lulu Mall'

Lulu Mall is part of the renowned Lulu Group International and has become a landmark in Kozhikode. Opened to much fanfare, it offers a comprehensive shopping and entertainment experience. Lulu Mall is known for its vast array of brands, including high-end fashion outlets, electronics, and a hypermarket that caters to everyday needs. The mall features a large food court with international and local food chains, along with various dining options. Entertainment facilities such as a multiplex theater, gaming zones, and event spaces make it a popular choice for family outings. Lulu Mall has brought a new level of retail sophistication to Kozhikode, and its central location makes it easily accessible.
Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 10:00 am – 10:00 pm
Saturday and Sunday: 09:00 am to 12:00 am

'Gokulam Galleria'

"Gokulam Mall, located in the heart of Kozhikode, is a relatively smaller mall but provides a well-rounded shopping experience. The mall offers a range of retail stores, including clothing, footwear, accessories, and electronics. Its layout is designed to provide visitors with a comfortable shopping experience. Gokulam Mall is also known for its food court, offering a variety of cuisines. The mall’s calm and spacious environment makes it an ideal spot for casual shopping and relaxation, attracting families and individuals looking for a less crowded shopping experience compared to the larger malls.
Opening Hours: Sunday to Saturday: 09:30 am – 11:00 pm

'Focus Mall'

"Focus Mall is one of the oldest and most established malls in Kozhikode. Located in the city center, it is a key shopping hub offering a variety of stores, from national and international fashion brands to electronics and accessories. Focus Mall also features a dedicated food court with a variety of eateries, making it a popular spot for dining and socializing. The mall’s multiplex cinema is a significant draw for moviegoers. Though it is smaller compared to some newer malls, Focus Mall remains a favorite due to its convenient location and range of options for shoppers.
Opening Hours: Sunday to Saturday: 10:00 am – 10:00 pm

'RP Mall'

"RP Mall in Kozhikode is the ultimate shopping destination, offering a diverse range of retail stores, dining options, and entertainment under one roof. Whether you're looking for trendy apparel, electronics, home décor, or the latest gadgets, you'll find it here with popular local and international brands. After a fun shopping spree, you can unwind at the food court, which serves a variety of delicious meals, from fast food to local delicacies. RP Mall also offers entertainment with its modern cinema, perfect for movie lovers. The mall is designed with families in mind, providing a safe and comfortable environment for all, with play areas for children and spacious facilities. Conveniently located in the heart of Kozhikode with ample parking, RP Mall promises a hassle-free experience, making it a must-visit spot for both locals and visitors alike.
Opening Hours: Sunday to Saturday: 07:00 am – 12:00 am
